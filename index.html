Advanced Regenerative Mesh for Surgical Excellence

Surgicoll-Mesh is a bio compatible collagen mesh that is flexible, strong yet soft collagen membrane that is supple when hydrated and handles like natural tissue; it readily conforms to the surgical site upon application and is easily sutured. Surgicoll-Mesh is a reconstituted type-I collagen mesh free of contaminants like lipids, elastin and other immunogenic proteins. The collagen is used in the Surgicoll-Mesh product is based on a patented technology that ensures the high purity type-I collagen. It is processed under GMP conditions with stringent USP quality tests as per WHO (World Health Organization) and other international standards.

Surgicoll-Mesh is Bio-compatible and sterilized Type-1 collogen matrix for soft tissue & muscle flap reinforcement, Dural repair, Colorectal, urethral & vaginal Prolapse, pelvic floor reconstruction , urethral sling & Third Degree Burn to accommodate the skin graft needs,knee replacement surgery & other orthopaedic procedures

Surgicoll-Mesh is an implantable, bioresorbable, sterile Type-I collagen matrix designed to support tissue regeneration, wound healing, and surgical reconstruction. It is manufactured using patented high-purity collagen technology for superior biocompatibility and clinical performance.

Unlike conventional collagen products, Surgicoll-Mesh contains ultra-pure Type-I collagen that is free from contaminants such as elastin, lipids, and immunogenic proteins. Its phosphorylated collagen structure enhances cellular signaling and promotes faster tissue regeneration.

The phosphorylated Type-I collagen helps attract cells, improve cell communication, and stimulate tissue remodeling. Clinical observations have shown neo-vascularization and capillary formation within 4 to 5 days after application, supporting faster healing and tissue integration.
